I WILL NOT LIE OR STEAL OR CHEAT

I WILL LEARN SOMETHING NEW EVERYDAY

I WILL TRY MY BEST TO GET ALONG WITH OTHERS

I WILL BE GRATEFUL FOR THE EXPERIENCE REGARDLESS

I WILL NOT FORCE MYSELF TO BELONG SOMEWHERE I DO NOT WANT TO BE

I WILL COMMIT MYSELF TO SOMETHING THAT MAKES ME HAPPY

I WILL QUESTION THAT HAPPINESS AND BE UNAFRAID TO START OVER IF IT IS NO LONGER FULFILLING

I WILL SPEAK KINDLY OF MYSELF AND OTHERS

I WILL PUT MYSELF IN UNCOMFORTABLE SITUATIONS AND TRUST THAT THE SOLUTIONS WILL COME

I WILL LOOK AND VALUE INWARDS

I WILL CONFESS TO MY CRIMES AND ADMIT MY SHORTCOMINGS

I WILL NO LONGER AVOID THE TAX I MUST PAY
