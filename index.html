About this website

  • home-this is where you are right now. hello!
  • articles- this is where i write about counterstrike. it's primarily focused on the pro scene- teams, players, matches, roster moves, or any other developments i find worth talking about.while it is mostly news structures, it's not strictly tied to what is happening right now.
  • essays- this is a section for the writings that don't fit as neatly into match summaries or stat sheets. writings here are more research driven- sometimes directly connected to counterstrike, sometimes only loosely connected. while primarily academic based, counterstrike serves as a framework for all of these writings.
  • data and stats-this section serves as a purpose for me to evolve and progress in the data analysis side of counterstrike. it is quite open ended as i am still shaping what this section will become, but right now it is a place for playing with stats and seeing what patterns can emerge.
  • surf logs-this is where i document my progress in surf, review maps, and showcase my own maps. i also have some hammer tutorials included here if interested in creating maps for any valve game.
  • personal-this section acts like a journal for me where i can write whatever i am thinking about at any time of the day. however, most of the time i am thinking about counter strike.
  • artwork-i like making art sometimes. if i like it enough i will put it here. if you are an artist or editor or make any type of art and would like to be featured here, you can message me. if i like it enough i will put it here.

feel free to contact me through twitter or email if you have specific post requests, questions, artwork to share, or you would just like to say hello
